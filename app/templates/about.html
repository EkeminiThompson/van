About Vanessa Curdles Care Foundation

Vanessa Curdles Care Foundation is dedicated to making a positive impact in communities through various initiatives and programs.

We focus on:

  • Advocacy for community rights and empowerment.
  • Agriculture development for sustainable livelihoods.
  • Capacity building to enhance skills and opportunities.
  • Care and support for those in need.
  • Career development to empower individuals.
  • Charity activities to support vulnerable groups.
  • Children issues to safeguard their rights and well-being.
